Frequently Asked Questions

You can earn SCUDO by recycling waste in the smart bins located around USJ campuses. Simply scan the QR code on the bin, select the type and quantity of waste, and deposit your recyclables. You can also earn larger amounts by bringing bulk recyclables to the designated EcoHub drop-off points.

SCUDO can be spent in the IGKNIGHT store to purchase USJ merchandise like clothing, accessories, and stationery. You can also invest your SCUDO in various campus club projects and initiatives, helping to fund sustainability efforts and activities that benefit the university community.

Smart bins are equipped with QR codes and electronic lids. To use them, scan the QR code with the IGKNIGHT app, select the type and quantity of waste you're recycling, and the bin will unlock so you can deposit your recyclables. After successful disposal, SCUDO will be automatically credited to your account.

We're always looking for volunteers to join our various teams! Whether you're interested in technology, finance, waste management, or outreach, there's a place for you. Contact us through the Support page or speak with the IGKNIGHT representatives in your faculty to learn about current volunteer opportunities.

The smart bins accept several categories of recyclable waste including plastic (bottles, containers), paper (notebooks, printouts), metal (cans, small metal items), and glass (bottles, jars). Each bin is clearly labeled with the types of waste it accepts. For electronic waste or larger items, please visit the dedicated EcoHub drop-off points.
